Casey Rogers Bio

Casey Rogers is a Western Washington Graphic Design student. Casey was born to Chris and Susan Rogers who currently live on Whidbey Island. She hopes to become a graphic designer for a tech company, with interest in accessibility design. In her younger school days she enjoyed Clubs such as ASB, Engine repair, Chess or Art Club. She has been playing Ice hockey since she was 5. She has one younger brother named Nikolas. Casey moved schools a lot growing up which means she has friends from all over.
Casey really loves her family and wants to take care of them and make them proud. When she gets the funds she will build a treehouse and retire in it. Casey Rogers looks forward to the future and hopes to make people smile with the things she makes.
